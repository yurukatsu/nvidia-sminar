## Abstract

Financial institutions are deploying LLMs for several use cases including trading, advisory, and consumer-facing applications—but opaque model behavior limits trust, especially in high-stakes situations. This session shows how mechanistic interpretability can reveal internal circuits and activations that drive predictions, making LLMs auditable and controllable. We’ll cover theory and practical applications across trading strategies, sentiment analysis, and hallucination reduction, linking interpretability not only to compliance and risk governance, but also to actionable trading signals.
